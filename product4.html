Geometric Kufic Panel

A modern abstract piece inspired by ancient Kufic geometric forms and patterns.

Product Details

This contemporary art piece reinterprets the timeless beauty of Kufic script through geometric abstraction. Clean lines and bold forms create a striking visual narrative, perfect for modern and minimalist spaces.

  • Dimensions: 30" x 30" (75cm x 75cm)
  • Materials: High-grade acrylic on stretched canvas, with a textured finish.
  • Artist: Karim Al-Din, Abstract Calligraphy Artist
  • Features intricate patterns inspired by classical Islamic geometry.
  • Comes with a sleek black floating frame, ready for display.
