Indic Script Scroll

A beautiful hand-painted scroll showcasing delicate Indic calligraphic artistry.

Product Details

Experience the rich heritage of the Indian subcontinent with this intricately hand-painted scroll. Featuring a devotional verse or a profound saying, this piece highlights the exquisite details and cultural depth of Indic calligraphy.

  • Dimensions: 15" x 40" (38cm x 100cm)
  • Materials: Traditional handmade paper, natural mineral pigments, wooden dowel rods.
  • Artist: Rakesh Sharma, Traditional Indian Calligrapher
  • Hand-rolled with decorative finials, presented in a protective silk pouch.
  • Ideal for spiritual spaces, cultural art collections, or as a unique gift.
